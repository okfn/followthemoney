#followthemoney

Follow the Money

We want citizens to be able to hold decision-makers to account for the use of public money, to ensure it meets their needs, isn't lost to fraud or corruption, and drives progress in crucial areas. In order for this to happen citizens must have access to information about where public money comes from, where it goes, and what it delivers.
